Black Hole Galaxy · Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Black Hole Galaxy (the “Game”, “we”, “us”), developed by lazaraGames (the “Developer”), handles information when you use our mobile game. By downloading or playing Black Hole Galaxy, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Black Hole Galaxy does not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information. We (the Developer) do not ourselves collect or store personal information about you.

However, the Game displays advertisements through Google AdMob, and that advertising service collects certain device and usage data in order to deliver and measure ads. See Section 4 for details.

2. Local Game Data

The Game saves your progress, settings, high scores, coins, and cosmetic choices locally on your device. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or to any third party, and is removed when you uninstall the Game.

3. Permissions

The Game may use your device’s vibration feature to provide haptic feedback during gameplay. This does not collect or transmit any data.

To deliver, personalize, measure, and protect these ads against fraud, AdMob and Google may collect and process certain information, including your device’s advertising identifier, IP address, approximate (non-precise) location derived from that IP address, general device information, and data about your interactions with ads.

Google processes this information in accordance with its own Privacy Policy and the “How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services” page. You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of ad personalization at any time in your device settings (on Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

The Game is also distributed through Google Play, which may collect data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.

Black Hole Galaxy does not offer in-app purchases and does not require an account. Advertising (Google AdMob) is the only third-party service the Game uses.

5. Children’s Privacy

Black Hole Galaxy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Data Security

The Developer does not operate its own servers and does not collect or store your personal data — your game progress, settings, and coins stay on your device. Any data collected through advertising is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections and is handled by Google under its own security and privacy practices.
